Gaël Aglin

I am a postdoctoral felow in Artificial Intelligence and Algorithms (AIA) research group at UCLouvain. I held a PhD from UCLouvain in 2022. My main research focuses on Machine Learning and Combinatorial Optimization. Broadly, I am passionate about programming and artificial intelligence; specifically creating machine learning models or applying deep learning algorithms on excting projects. I am also interested in solving combinatorial optimization problems using algorithmic techniques and tailored data structures. I like to discover and analyze new problems.
