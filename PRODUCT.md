# PRODUCT.md — aglingael.github.io

> Context file for the `impeccable` design skill. Source of truth for who this is for, the voice, and what to avoid.

## Register

`brand` — this is a personal portfolio. The design IS the product; a visitor's impression is the thing being made.

## Product purpose

The personal website of **Gaël Aglin, Ph.D.** — a Senior Data Scientist (Production ML · Decision Science · GenAI). It exists to:

1. Make a senior recruiter or hiring manager (Amazon / Google / Zalando tier) trust, within 10 seconds, that this is a rigorous builder of production decision systems, not a notebook tinkerer.
2. Give academic peers a clean entry point to the research (optimal decision trees, PyDL8.5) and publications.
3. Carry one memorable idea: **optimal decisions under constraints** — the throughline from his AAAI 2020 work to production pricing systems.

## Users

- **Primary:** senior tech recruiters and hiring managers for applied/research DS roles. Skim-first, credibility-sensitive, allergic to fluff.
- **Secondary:** ML researchers and open-source users (PyDL8.5), looking for papers and code.
- **Tertiary:** potential collaborators / clients (rendoc.africa).

## Brand voice — three physical-object words

**Precise · Structural · Quietly confident.** A well-machined scientific instrument. A technical specimen sheet. A research monograph set with intent. Substance over noise.

## Strategic principles

- **Earned, not loud.** 8+ years and measured impact speak for themselves. No growth-hack energy.
- **Show the work as the imagery.** The hero is a real artifact of the work (an optimal decision tree), not stock photography or a headshot we do not have.
- **Data is set as data.** Public metrics (citations, h-index, releases) are typeset in a mono with tabular figures, like instrument readouts.
- **One accent, used with reason.** Color marks the "optimal path" — it means something, it is not decoration.

## Confidentiality (hard constraint)

Some figures in the source CV are employer-linked business impact and must NOT appear on
the public site. (This file ships in the repo, so the rule is stated without restating any
confidential number.)

- **REMOVE** the headline mortgage-cohort lifetime-value amount (BNP Paribas Fortis) and any
  monetary business-impact figure tied to an employer.
- **REFRAME** that impact qualitatively: "A/B-validated production dynamic-pricing framework
  with significant, measured business impact."
- **KEEP** public metrics: citation counts, h-index, open-source release/commit counts,
  competition placements, paper venues. These are already public.

## Anti-references (what this must NOT look like)

- The data-scientist reflex: dark UI + electric blue accent + monospace-everything. First-order AI slop.
- The editorial-magazine reflex: display-serif italic + drop caps + ruled three-column broadsheet. Second-order slop.
- A generic SaaS template: centered hero, icon-title-subtitle card grid, gradient hero-metric band.
- A bootcamp portfolio: project cards with screenshots and "I'm passionate about data."
